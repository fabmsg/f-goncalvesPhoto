Meet Filipe

I’m a photographer and visual storyteller rooted in Porto, Portugal. The Atlantic horizon has shaped my creative life – from childhood memories spent on the coast to editorial assignments that now take me across the globe.

My approach balances documentary authenticity with a painterly sensibility. Whether I’m working on portraits, travel essays, or commercial campaigns, I focus on presence, empathy, and respect for the communities I photograph.

Philosophy

Photography is about listening before pressing the shutter. I spend time learning about the people and places I photograph, allowing for organic collaboration and meaningful storytelling. Natural light, texture, and subtle colour grading are pillars of my visual language.

“I believe in photographs that feel like memories – honest, poetic, and layered.”
