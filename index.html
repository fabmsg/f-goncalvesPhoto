Atlantic light. Human stories. Crafted photographs.

I’m Filipe Gonçalves, a photographer focused on luminous coastal landscapes, intimate portraiture, and visual narratives that honour people and place. My portfolio highlights work from across Portugal, Europe, and the Atlantic – always with a painterly feel and a love for texture and light.
